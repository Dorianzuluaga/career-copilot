# Purpose

This document defines the identity of Career Copilot.

It establishes the product's mission, vision, principles, and long-term direction. Every product decision, feature, and technical implementation should align with the ideas described here.

This is the highest-level product document and serves as the foundation for all future specifications.

---

# Product Identity

Career Copilot is an AI-powered Career Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps people organize, optimize, and automate every stage of their job search.

Instead of replacing the user's decisions, it reduces repetitive work, centralizes information, and provides intelligent assistance throughout the entire career application journey.

Career Copilot is not a job board.

It is a career management platform.

---

# Mission

Help job seekers spend less time managing the job search process and more time getting interviews.

---

# Vision

Become the essential AI companion for anyone looking to grow, change, or advance their career.

Career Copilot aims to centralize every important aspect of the job search into one intelligent platform.

---

# Problem Statement

Searching for a job requires managing dozens of repetitive and disconnected tasks.

Users frequently:

- lose track of applications
- adapt the same CV repeatedly
- write similar cover letters
- forget where they applied
- struggle to prepare interviews
- use multiple tools to manage the process

As the number of applications grows, so does the cognitive load.

The job search becomes a full-time job itself.

---

# Target User

Primary users are professionals actively searching for employment who want to improve the quality, organization, and efficiency of their job search.

Typical users:

- Junior Developers
- Career Changers
- Recent Graduates
- Experienced Professionals
- Freelancers looking for full-time opportunities

---

# Value Proposition

Career Copilot centralizes, automates, and optimizes the complete job search process using Artificial Intelligence.

Instead of switching between multiple tools, users manage everything from one workspace.

---

# Product Principles

The product is guided by the following principles:

1. Simplicity over complexity.
2. AI assists, users decide.
3. Never ask twice for information already provided.
4. Every automation must save real time.
5. Minimize repetitive tasks.
6. Keep every application organized.
7. Reduce cognitive load.
8. Prioritize usability over feature quantity.

---

# Product Pillars

Career Copilot is built around four pillars:

### 1. Organization

Centralize every job application, document, recruiter, interview, and note.

### 2. Optimization

Use AI to improve CVs, cover letters, and application quality.

### 3. Automation

Reduce repetitive work through intelligent workflows.

### 4. Guidance

Provide contextual assistance during every stage of the hiring process.

---

# Product Differentiators

Career Copilot differentiates itself by:

- AI-first experience
- End-to-end career management
- Centralized application tracking
- Intelligent document generation
- Focus on reducing cognitive workload
- Designed around real job seekers instead of recruiters

---

# MVP North Star

The first version of the product should allow a user to:

1. Store a Master CV.
2. Paste a job description.
3. Generate an optimized CV.
4. Generate a personalized cover letter.
5. Save the application.
6. Track the application status.

If these six actions are completed successfully, the MVP has achieved its primary objective.

---

# Success Metrics

The MVP will be considered successful if users can:

- reduce application preparation time
- maintain organized application tracking
- generate high-quality application documents
- prepare more applications with less effort

---

# Product Philosophy

We are not building another platform to search for jobs.

We are building a platform that makes searching for a job feel less like one.

---

# Out of Scope (MVP)

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP:

- Job marketplace
- Social network
- Recruiter management system
- Video interview platform
- Internal messaging system
- Enterprise recruiting features

These may be considered in future versions but are not required for validating the product.

---

# Future Evolution

Career Copilot is designed to evolve into a complete AI-powered career management ecosystem.

Potential future capabilities include:

- AI Interview Coach
- Career Advisor
- Salary Negotiation Assistant
- LinkedIn Optimization
- Networking Assistant
- Learning Roadmaps
- Career Analytics
- Multi-agent collaboration
